Spicy Curry

A dish composed with the buttermilk or curd gravy seasoned with a mixture of ground spices that is thought to have originated in India and has since spread to many regions of the world.

Carrot Pudding

It is a carrot-based sweet dessert pudding made by placing grated carrots in a pot containing a specific amount of water, milk and sugar, cardamom and then cooking while stirring regularly. It is often served with a garnish of almonds and pistachios

Potato Prantha

Aloo paratha is a (flat bread dish) stuffed with potato filling native to the Indian subcontinent.It is made using unleavened dough rolled with a mixture of mashed potato and spices (amchur, garam masala) which is cooked on a hot tawa with butter or ghee, Aloo paratha is usually served with butter, chutney, curd, or Indian pickles.
